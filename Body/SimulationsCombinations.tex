%Simulation Dataset Overview. Focus on Auger (simulations with updated hadronic interaction models)

With the recently deployed detectors of AugerPrime, the Pierre Auger Observatory simultaneously measures several, complimentary, air-shower observables for primary cosmic ray determination.
From the Heitler-Matthews model (see~\Cref{chap:airshowers}), the reconstructed observables are only statistically related to the mass of the primary cosmic ray.
On average, there is a correlation between these observables and cosmic-ray mass, yet shower-to-shower fluctuations complicate mass determination at an event level.
